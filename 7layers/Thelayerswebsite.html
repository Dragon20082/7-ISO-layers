You are probly wondering what the 7 ISO layers are. I don't blame you.

The 7 ISO layers are the 7 leves of the Open System Interconnection model

  1. 1 Pysical; Physical Structure
  2. 2 data link; Frames
  3. 3 Network; Packets
  4. 4 Transport; End-to-end connection
  5. 5 The Session Layer
  6. 6 Presentation; Syntax layer
  7. 7 Application; End user layer
